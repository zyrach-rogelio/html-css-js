Project Overview:

The World of Ardonia (Working Title) is one of the most detailed writing projects I have currently, it currently has only a master document that contains all of the important lore and history of the world I am building. The master document includes:

Key Distinctions to other works of fiction:
  • The World has a magic tier system
  • The World is governed by pseudo science based magic
  • There are two types of people in this world, the Conduits (Which can only absorb magic) and the Manipulators (Which can only manipulate and shape Magic)

  • The Domain system that governs the cosmic principles of this world
  • The Blight (Which is the antagonist of this setting)

Tensura Learning Mod!
Project Overview

There isnt too much to work with, however the concept of the unique skill that I wanted to implement in the Minecraft mod is very polished, there were Three evolution paths for this Unique skill, it was:

3 Very Fleshed out Unique Skills with its Kits:
  • The First in line: Processor
  • The Second in line: Paragon
  • And the Ultimate Skill version: Prometheus, Titan of Progress
The explanations are too lengthy so let me summarize what they do:

Processor: Allows the player to summon clones that will help in learning skills faster!

Paragon: It does the same stuff as its previous version however it gets upgraded by allowing the player to manipulate Matter and Magicules!

Prometheus, Titan of Progress: It basically allows the player to master Magicules and Molecules at same time!
